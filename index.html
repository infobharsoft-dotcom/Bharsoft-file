One secure hub for every website you run.

Bharsoft connects independent websites into a single trusted ecosystem — each site keeps its own backend and data, while Bharsoft handles discovery, secure handshakes, and relay between them.

What Bharsoft actually does
Ecosystem hubRegisters every member site and lets them discover each other's capabilities without exposing internal systems.
Secure handshakeEach connection between two sites gets its own signed secret — nothing is trusted by URL alone.
Relay layerOne site can ask another to run an action (like sending mail) through Bharsoft, without ever seeing its backend.
Session-based accessEvery admin action is checked against a live, expiring session — not a static key.
Admin controlServices, members, and access can be managed from one panel, kept in sync across the public site.

A platform that connects your ecosystem — not one that owns it

Bharsoft is a platform that provides the ecosystem layer for multiple independent websites. Each site keeps its own backend, its own data, and its own login — Bharsoft only handles the secure communication between them: discovery, trust, and relay.

That means adding a new site to your ecosystem doesn't mean merging systems or handing over access. It means a signed, one-directional handshake that only shares what's needed.

About Bharsoft

Built as connective tissue, not a walled garden

Bharsoft exists to let independent websites talk to each other safely — without any one of them giving up ownership of their data, users, or infrastructure.

🧩

Independent by design

Every member keeps its own backend, database, and authentication. Bharsoft never becomes a single point of failure for your data.

🔐

Trust is explicit

A member only trusts another after a deliberate, signed handshake — never automatically, never by default.

📡

Capability discovery

Sites announce what they can do ("send mail", "process payment") without exposing how they do it internally.
